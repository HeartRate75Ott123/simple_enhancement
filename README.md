## Gear & Items

- **Netherite Diamond Set** — A full tier of armor and tools crafted from Netherite Diamond Alloy
- **Upgrade Orb (LV1–6)** — Upgrade your sword's base stats via an anvil
- **Reflective Shield** — Blocks and reflects incoming projectiles
- **Confusion Charge** — Throwable projectile that disorients enemies
- **Mutagen** — Grants temporary combat mutations
- **Smithing Templates** — Basic and Advanced templates for gear progression

## World Loot

Enchanted books and upgrade orbs spawn in structure chests throughout all vanilla tiers — from dungeons and shipwrecks to Nether fortresses and End cities.
