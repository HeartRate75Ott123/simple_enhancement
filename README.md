# Simple Enhancement

A lightweight Minecraft 1.21.1 NeoForge mod that expands combat with new enchantments, gear, and a sword upgrade system.

## Features

### 13 New Enchantments
All available as enchanted books from structure chests across every vanilla tier — from dungeons to End cities. Each enchantment is sword-compatible:

| Enchantment | Max Level | Effect |
|-------------|-----------|--------|
| Reach Strike | III | Extends melee attack range |
| Swift Strike | III | Increases attack speed |
| Heavy Strike | III | Adds bonus damage per hit |
| Guard Strike | III | Grants damage resistance on kill |
| Weaken Strike | III | Inflicts Weakness on enemies |
| All-In Strike | I | Massive damage at durability cost |
| Ender Pulse | III | Teleports attacker to target |
| Delay Slash | I | Delayed damage burst |
| Sacrificial Slash | III | Bonus damage at health cost |
| Hunger Slash | III | Restores hunger on kill |
| Wave Swift | I | Bonus damage underwater |
| Life Absorb | III | Life steal on hit |
| Mob Friend | I | Chance to pacify hostile mobs |

### Upgrade Orb System (LV1–6)
Use an Upgrade Orb and a Diamond Block in the crafting table to upgrade your sword. Combine the orb with a sword in an anvil to permanently boost base damage. Higher orb tiers drop from tougher structures.

### New Items
- **Netherite Diamond Alloy** — Advanced crafting material
- **Netherite Diamond Gear** — Full armor and tool set
- **Reflective Shield** — Deflects projectiles
- **Confusion Charge** — Throwable disorientation projectile
- **Enhanced Confusion Charge** — Larger, stronger variant
- **Mutagen** — Temporary combat mutation potion
- **Basic & Advanced Smithing Templates** — Gear progression

### World Integration
Enchanted books and upgrade orbs spawn naturally in structure chests: dungeons, shipwrecks, desert pyramids, jungle temples, pillager outposts, trial chambers, Nether fortresses, bastions, woodland mansions, and End cities.

## Compatibility
- **Minecraft**: 1.21.1
- **Mod Loader**: NeoForge 21.1.190+
- **Side**: Both (client and server)
